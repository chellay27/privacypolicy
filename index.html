About Our Apps

Sharmila Yoga Zone (SYZ)

Sharmila Yoga Zone is an app designed to bring the benefits of yoga to everyone. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced practitioner, our app provides customized yoga sessions, guided meditations, and a supportive community to help you on your journey to wellness.

Learn more about the SYZ app by visiting the SYZ Privacy Policy.

Mealpool

Mealpool is an app that focuses on sharing and selling food within an office environment. It allows users to share home-cooked meals or sell food to colleagues, fostering a community of food lovers and making meal times more enjoyable and convenient.

Learn more about the Mealpool app by visiting the Mealpool Privacy Policy.
